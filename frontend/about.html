About CounselBot

CounselBot is a research project developing a transformer-based chatbot for mental health support. This study aims to enhance mental health accessibility in the Philippines, where over 11% of the population is affected by mental health disorders, by providing an empathetic, context-aware conversational AI system.

Technical Approach

The system utilizes fine-tuned transformer-based models, specifically LLaMA 3.1 and BERT. The chatbot processes user messages through multiple components: emotion classification, response generation, and mental health analysis, creating a comprehensive support system.

Privacy & Ethics

Your privacy is a priority. All conversations are kept confidential and are not stored permanently.

Note: CounselBot is not a replacement for professional mental health services. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services or a mental health professional.
