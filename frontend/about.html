About CounselBot

CounselBot is an AI-powered mental health companion designed to provide support and guidance through conversation. Built with empathy and understanding, it aims to create a safe space for discussing mental health concerns.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves access to mental health support. CounselBot is designed to be a first step in seeking help, providing a judgment-free environment for expressing thoughts and feelings.

How It Works

Using advanced natural language processing, CounselBot engages in meaningful conversations, offering support and guidance while maintaining confidentiality. It's designed to complement professional mental health services, not replace them.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. All conversations are kept confidential and are not stored permanently. We use secure protocols to ensure your data remains protected.

Note: CounselBot is not a replacement for professional mental health services. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services or a mental health professional.
